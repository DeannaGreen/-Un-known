Leicester Square

All these locations are within 1 mile of London Bridge



Second Hand Book Shopping

Distance from Tower Bridge

(67 Reviews)

• Charing Cross Road has 3 Second hand bookshops on one parade: Any Amount of Books, Henry Pordes and Quinto’s Bookshop (based in the basement of Francis Edwards Antique and Rare books)
• Most books £4 and under
• Any Amount of Books periodically has basement sales where any book Is £1 or 5 books for £4 - check their social media for basement sale announcements if interested
• Quinto’s restocks completely each month

Live Music Every Day in Covent Garden

Distance from Tower Bridge

(67 Reviews)

• Either opera or musicians live every day in the shopping complex between the streets and the Jubilee market, one level below street level
• Not particularly crowded
• Good acoustics

House of MinaLima

Distance from Tower Bridge

(67 Reviews)

• 4 floors - of exhibitions of the work by the graphic designers of the Warner Brothers Harry Potter and Fantastic Beasts franchises and 1 floor gift shop
• See props, designs, artwork and photos from both Fantastic Beasts and Where to find them and the Harry Potter Franchises
• Gift Shop with signed merchandise and prop replicas as well as limited edition prints
• Miraphora MIna and Eduardo Lima (the graphic designers) often pop in and surprise visitors

Golden Square

Distance from Tower Bridge

(67 Reviews)

• Square gardens with lawns and shrubs
• Statue of George II on a plinth - one of the only two public statues of George II in the capital
• Thought to have been laid out according to plans by Sir Christopher Wren, around the year 1670s

Forbidden Planet London

Distance from Tower Bridge

(67 Reviews)

• Megastore from the world’s largest and best-known science fiction, fantasy and cult entertainment retailer
